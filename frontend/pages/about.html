Who We Are

A Legacy Built on
Land & Loyalty

What began as a vision to make premium land ownership accessible has grown into one of Pune's most trusted real estate names.

Founded in Pune, Samarth Properties started with a simple conviction: every family deserves a plot of land they can truly call their own. Over the years we have delivered multiple projects — from government-approved residential plots near Ranjangaon MIDC to weekend home destinations across the Pune district.

Our approach is rooted in transparency. Every land parcel we offer is legally verified, RERA compliant where applicable, and backed by our after-sale support. We don't just sell land — we walk alongside our customers through the entire ownership journey.
